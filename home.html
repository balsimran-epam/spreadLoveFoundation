Mission

Discover and support the less privileged individuals for well-being of the society.
    Providing critical needs for the orphans, being authentic to our responsibilities.
    Being grateful to the staff, volunteers and donors who make it look possible.
    The goal is to build happiness, generate it globally and handover the assignment
    from generation to generation.

Vision

We believe when you change a childs' life, you change a community which
will eventually change the world.
SLF makes every effort to join the solution in fighting poverty and improving
the lives of the less privileged.
This is a policy we ought to continue and we hope to be more efficient than ever.
